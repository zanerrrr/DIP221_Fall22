Why are cats so adorable and lovable?

Since the ancient times in Egypt, cats have been one of the most royal animals in the world. And since then they have been loved and respected and have become the most popular pet. Cats are wild animsls and over the yeares they have been domesticated. According to this article there are more than 40 cat breeds that have been recognized by different cat associations. Cats have earned the respect. They are known for their spiky claws and strong teeth. Did you know that cats do not get cavities? Now you would likle to be a cat yourself.

Even though not everyone likes cats, they just don't really care. The reason why cats have been so loved through the centuries of human history is unknow. Maybe there's come kind of connection between the ancient Egypt and Cleopatra or the fact that cats are royal and unforgivable balls of cuteness.
